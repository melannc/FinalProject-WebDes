Frequently Asked Questions

Our studio was founded in 2024 with a mission to create immersive and innovative gaming experiences. We started with a small team and have grown significantly over the years. Our first major release was Image Horizon, which established our reputation in the industry.

We specialize in fighting games, but we also explore other genres. Our goal is to deliver unique and engaging experiences across various styles.

We develop games for many platforms, including PC, consoles, and mobile devices. We also explore emerging platforms and technologies as they become available.

Our development process involves several phases: concept development, pre-production, production, testing, and post-launch support. We use agile methodologies to adapt and refine our projects throughout the development cycle.

Our mission is to create memorable and enjoyable gaming experiences that push the boundaries of creativity and technology. We aim to craft games that resonate with players and leave a lasting impact.
